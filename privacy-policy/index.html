Tracking

Zellous Design tracks visitor information in order to understand how people are finding this site, how well it's meeting their needs, and if there are any code bugs that need squashing. If you'd rather not have information related to your visit tracked, here's how you can opt-out:

Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout/.

To opt-out of Hotjar Insights, you'll need to contact Hotjar.

(Alternatively, you could protect your privacy by using a browser extension such as Ghostery)
